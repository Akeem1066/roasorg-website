Frequently Asked Questions

Can I recover my account after deletion?

No, account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All data is permanently removed from our servers.

How long does the deletion process take?

Account deletion is processed within 24 hours of your request. You'll receive a confirmation email once completed.

Will my data be shared with third parties?

No, we do not share, sell, or transfer your personal data to third parties. All data is handled according to our privacy policy.

What if I change my mind after requesting deletion?

If you contact us within 24 hours of your deletion request, we may be able to cancel the process. After 24 hours, the deletion cannot be reversed.

Is my data backed up anywhere?

No, we do not maintain backup copies of user data. Once deleted, all information is permanently removed from our systems.
